Create a beautiful Android and iOS mobile application called "Our Hearts" designed exclusively for two people in a long-distance relationship.

Core Concept

This app is a private emotional space where only two users can connect, chat, and share their feelings through text messages, emojis, and heartfelt notes. The app should feel warm, romantic, peaceful, and emotionally comforting.

Design & Theme
Primary color: Soft Light Pink (#FFD6E7)
Secondary colors: White, Cream, and Pastel Rose
Minimalist and elegant UI
Smooth animations
Calm and peaceful aesthetic
Rounded corners on cards and buttons
Soft shadows
Romantic but mature design (not childish)
Beautiful typography
Cozy and emotional atmosphere
Features
1. Private Couple Space
Only two registered users can join.
Secure login and authentication.
Invite code system so only the partner can enter.
2. Couple Chat Room
One shared chat room.
Send and receive:
Text messages
Emojis
Heart reactions ❤️
Cute stickers
Real-time messaging.
Typing indicator.
Read receipts ("Seen").
3. Feelings Journal
Users can write long emotional notes.
Save memories and feelings.
Notes can include emojis.
Beautiful card-based layout.
4. Daily Love Notes
Send one special daily message.
Highlighted in a separate section.
Animated heart effect when opened.
5. Mood Sharing
Choose mood:
Happy 😊
Missing You 🥺
Loving ❤️
Sad 😔
Excited 😍
Partner can instantly see current mood.
6. Memory Timeline
Store important memories.
Add title, date, and description.
Timeline view with soft animations.
7. Relationship Counter
Show:
Days together
Months together
Years together
Beautiful romantic dashboard.
8. Notifications
Notify partner when:
New message arrives
New feeling note is shared
Mood changes
Daily love note is sent

Goal

The application should feel like a private digital love diary and messaging space where two people can comfortably express feelings they may find difficult to share on social media or phone calls. The overall experience should be emotionally warm, peaceful, romantic, secure, and distraction-free.
